Sustainability

Will Knock Your Socks Off

Our Premise

The goals we set for our group is to put together information on Georgia Tech’s sustainability on campus, including ideas such as water, electricity, recycling, and compare this information with the ideas of individuals on Georgia Tech’s campus in the form of a survey.

The Survey

We conducted a survey of our peers at Georgia Tech on their daily habits. We asked them questions on how long they shower, how often they use air conditioning etc.

Reasearch: Electricity

We conducted research on Georgia Tech's electricity consumption as well as where this electricity comes from. In addition, we collected data on other schools to see how Georgia Tech compares to them when it comes to using less energy.
